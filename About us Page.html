The story of SEA SIDE PARK HOTEL Colombo which opened its doors in 1984 is a splendid
tale of continual improvement of product and the highest standards of quality in hospitality.

Having embraced over 3 decades of expertise in hospitality our vision and beliefs are
firmly grounded in extending a true personalized service to all our guests, laced with
an unforgettable luxury hotel experience.

The brand has enticed many elite personalities from around the world including heads
of government, prime ministers of leading nations, royalty, well known sports and
entertainment personalities and many more.
